'The Apostrophe' Graphic Design

Project Description

Graphic designer for the student-lead magazine ‘The Apostrophe,’ “by the Students, for the People,” which highlights SOU events and courses as well as scenery from around Ashland, OR.

Utilized Adobe Creative Suite to design visually compelling newsletter & magazine layouts. Formatted copy and data into readable sections of text.

Applied knowledge of color theory and typography to create themes that complemented the information being conveyed.
